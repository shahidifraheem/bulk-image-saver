Privacy Policy for Bulk Image Saver

Last Updated: July 18, 2025

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All image processing happens locally in your browser.

1. Information We Don't Collect

This extension does not collect or transmit:

  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • Personal identification information
  • Image content beyond temporary processing
  • Any data about your device or location

2. How Your Data is Processed

When you use Bulk Image Saver:

  • Image scanning occurs only on your current tab when you activate the extension
  • All processing happens locally in your browser - no data is sent to servers
  • Downloaded images go directly to your chosen download folder

3. Permission Justifications

Permission Purpose
activeTab Access only the webpage you're currently viewing when you click the extension
downloads Save selected images to your local device
scripting Scan the webpage DOM for images (required for Manifest V3 extensions)

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced via:

  • The Chrome Web Store update notes
  • A notification in the extension (if applicable)
